Types of Pollution

Air Pollution: This occurs when harmful substances are released into the air, affecting the quality of the air we breathe.

Water Pollution: Contamination of water bodies by pollutants, making it unsafe for aquatic life and human consumption.

Soil Contamination: The presence of hazardous substances in the soil, negatively impacting plant growth and contaminating the food chain.
