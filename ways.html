Ways to Reduce Pollution

1. Reduce, Reuse, Recycle: Minimize waste by following the three Rs to decrease the amount of garbage that ends up in landfills.

2. Use Sustainable Transportation: Opt for eco-friendly modes of transportation, such as walking, cycling, or using public transport.

3. Support Clean Energy: Promote and use renewable energy sources like solar and wind power to reduce dependence on fossil fuels.
